Natural Spring-Fed Water

Flows Straight From The Roaring River Into Our Resorts

All the water used at Sandals Dunn’s River and Sandals Royal Dunn’s River, including brand-new water elements throughout the resorts, will be sourced from the mineral-rich spring water of the Roaring River. The Dunn’s River Falls are a living testament to Jamaica’s natural beauty and are continuously rebuilt by the sediments in spring water year after year.
